01

Who We Are

MonetStudy ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-native learning platform that allows students and learners worldwide to upload their own study materials — PDFs, presentations, and notes — and receive AI-generated course modules, quizzes, flashcards, audio overviews, and personalised mock exams.

MonetStudy is operated by KNEITHEN TECH, based in Kampala, Uganda. You can reach us at the contact details in Section 11 of this policy.

By accessing MonetStudy at monetstudy.vercel.app or through our Android application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

03

How We Use Your Data

We use your data strictly to provide and improve MonetStudy:

  • → To authenticate your account and personalise your experience
  • → To process your uploaded files and generate AI course content
  • → To process subscription payments and maintain billing records
  • → To improve platform performance, fix bugs, and develop new features
  • → To send you service-related notifications (account activity, billing)
  • → To comply with legal obligations
We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your uploaded study materials to train AI models. We do not display third-party advertising.

04

Third-Party Services

MonetStudy uses trusted third-party services to operate. Each receives only the data necessary for their specific function:

  • Google Firebase Authentication, database storage, and file hosting — google.com/firebase
  • Google Auth Sign-in via your Google account (OAuth 2.0) — accounts.google.com
  • DeepSeek AI language model used to generate course content from your uploads — deepseek.com
  • Pesapal Payment processing for subscriptions (Uganda Shillings and other currencies) — pesapal.com
  • Vercel Web hosting and serverless infrastructure — vercel.com

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies and data processing agreements. We encourage you to review their policies for full details.

05

AI Processing & Your Content

When you upload a file to MonetStudy, its content is sent to the DeepSeek API for processing. This is what powers the generation of your course modules, quizzes, flashcards, and mock exams.

Your uploaded content is processed by DeepSeek solely for the purpose of generating your personalised learning materials. We do not use your content to train any AI model, and we do not share it with any other party for any other purpose.

Courses you create are private by default. If you use the "Make Public" toggle to share a course, that course becomes visible to other MonetStudy users. You can make a public course private again at any time from your dashboard.

Audio overviews generated from your materials are stored in Firebase Storage and associated with your account. They are not shared with other users unless you explicitly make the course public.

06

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active, or as long as necessary to provide our services.

Data type Retention period
Account & profile data Until account deletion
Uploaded files Until you delete them or your account is deleted
Generated course content Until you delete the course or your account
Payment records 7 years (required for financial compliance)
Usage logs 90 days

When you delete your account, your personal data and uploaded content are permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely and cannot be linked back to you.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion Request deletion of your account and all associated personal data
  • Portability Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection Object to processing where we rely on legitimate interests as a legal basis
  • Withdrawal Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 11. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

MonetStudy is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account on MonetStudy, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account and associated data.

In jurisdictions where the age of digital consent is higher (e.g. 16 in parts of the EU), users below that age must have verifiable parental consent before using the platform.

09

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. These include HTTPS encryption for all data in transit, Firebase Security Rules to restrict database and storage access, and OAuth 2.0 for authentication — meaning we never store your Google password.

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users promptly and take immediate steps to contain and remediate the incident.
